Frequently Asked Questions

How can I reset my password ?

To reset your password, go to the login page, click on the "Forgot Password" link, and follow the instructions sent to your registered email address.

How do I update my contact?

To update your contact information, log in to your account, navigate to the profile or account settings, and make the necessary changes. Don't forget to save the updated information.

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk purchases. Please contact our sales team or visit the pricing page for more information on available discounts and pricing plans..
